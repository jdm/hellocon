Hello, Con!

Hello, Con! is a computer science conference held at University of Toronto.

The goal of the conference is to provide a platform for sharing interesting programming experiences and ideas, expose participants to the real experience of being software developers, and above all, foster and extend the passion for computer science/computer engineering.

It will be comprised of a series of lightning talks given by selected speakers. Each talk will be on a different topic in the field of programming and computer science. Speakers will be sharing their personal stories, ongoing or completed projects, work experience, research results, or even future visions regarding the field of programming or computer science in a broader context. We welcome and encourage anyone interested in programming to attend!

Important! For anyone attending, please read and refer to the Code of Conduct for formal propriety of the conference.
