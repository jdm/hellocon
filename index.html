Hello, Con!

Hello, Con! is a conference at University of Toronto comprised of a series of lightning talks given by selected speakers. The talks are related to programming and computer science, but are otherwise unrestricted in terms of topic and form. Speakers are going to share their personal stories, ongoing or completed projects, work experience, research results, or even future visions regarding the field of programming or computer science in a broader context. We welcome all Computer Science and Engineering students as well as any developers from related industry fields who are interested.
