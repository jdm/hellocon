Submission

Submit before Jan 9, 2017

Speakers are encouraged to talk about their programming projects, work/research experience, or even future visions on the trend of programming. They are welcome to implement demos or use any tools as presentation aids. The time constraint for each speech is 10 minutes.

Each speaker must submit a request of proposal that includes the following information:

  • Title and abstract (will be displayed to the public)
  • Speech outline (content of presentation and use of special equipment, will only be used by the conference committee.)
  • Material needed (AV, computer, extra microphones)
  • Contact information
  • Permission for video recording and live streaming
